What is CRS Conclave?

CRS Conclave 2.0 is Gujarat's premier interschool technology and business event, bringing together the brightest young minds from across the region. This two-day extravaganza combines cutting-edge technology workshops, entrepreneurship challenges, and innovative competitions.

Our event serves as a launchpad for students to explore emerging technologies, develop business acumen, and network with industry leaders, creating an ecosystem where innovation thrives.
